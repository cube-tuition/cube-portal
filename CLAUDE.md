## Question bank — content rules (IMPORTANT)

When creating or adding questions to the question bank (the `qbank_*` tables), NEVER
reuse, copy, or closely replicate any question that appears in the curriculum
booklets/workbooks — including the PDFs attached to booklets in the database and any
PDFs/workbooks attached in chat. Students practise directly from those booklets, so
exam/worksheet questions must be original.

- Booklets may be used ONLY to learn the topics, skills, difficulty level, and question
  style for a class — never as a source of questions.
- Do not just swap the numbers in a booklet question; create genuinely new contexts and
  scenarios.
- This applies to every year level and class (e.g. Year 8 Amber, Year 9 Kevin) and to
  both MCQ and extended/short-answer questions.
